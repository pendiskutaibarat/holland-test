## Category Descriptions and Activities

### Outdoor

Interest in physical activities in open environments, nature, plants, animals,
and outdoor exploration.

Recommended activities:

- Gardening or school farming.
- Hiking or nature exploration.
- Scouting.
- Fishing.
- Outdoor sports.

### Mechanical & Practical

Interest in assembling, repairing, understanding machines, and creating
functional objects by hand.

Recommended activities:

- Robotics or basic electronics.
- Woodworking.
- Automotive exploration.
- Model or miniature assembly.
- Functional crafts.

### Computational & Clerical

Interest in structure, numbers, data, calculation, classification,
administration, and organized records.

Recommended activities:

- Bullet journaling.
- Chess or strategic board games.
- Basic coding.
- Collecting and classification.
- Finance or treasurer roles.

### Scientific

Interest in natural phenomena, experiments, observation, biology, astronomy, and
evidence-based inquiry.

Recommended activities:

- Independent science experiments.
- Amateur astronomy.
- Aquascape or terrarium building.
- Natural specimen collection.
- Science documentaries or encyclopedias.

### Persuasive

Interest in influencing others, public speaking, negotiation, debate,
leadership, and team organization.

Recommended activities:

- Debate or public speaking.
- Entrepreneurship projects.
- Opinion podcasting or vlogging.
- Event organizing.
- Student organization roles.

### Aesthetic

Interest in visual beauty, color, composition, design, photography, decoration,
and visual arts.

Recommended activities:

- Drawing and painting.
- Photography or videography.
- Graphic design.
- Fashion or styling.
- Decoration and room arrangement.

### Literary

Interest in text, storytelling, vocabulary, language, writing, reading, and
literature.

Recommended activities:

- Fiction writing.
- Book reviews or book clubs.
- Poetry and reflective journaling.
- Journalism or school magazine work.
- Foreign language learning.

### Musical

Interest in melody, rhythm, harmony, music performance, music appreciation, and
audio creativity.

Recommended activities:

- Playing musical instruments.
- Singing or choir.
- Digital music arrangement.
- Curated music listening.
- Songwriting.

### Social Service

Interest in helping others, mentoring, volunteering, listening, teaching, and
reducing other people's burdens.

Recommended activities:

- Humanitarian volunteering.
- Peer counseling.
- Tutoring or mentoring.
- Orphanage or elderly home visits.
- Social or environmental campaigns.

### Medical

Interest in human anatomy, basic health care, first aid, nutrition, fitness, and
disease prevention.

Recommended activities:

- Youth Red Cross or first aid training.
- Basic anatomy and health learning.
- Herbal medicine garden.
- Fitness and physical wellness.
- Nutrition planning.
